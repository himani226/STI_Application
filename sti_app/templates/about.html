About Us

Genesis

The Government of Punjab (GoP) is taking focused initiatives to make Punjab a Hub of Science Technology and Innovation. The state-of-the-art scientific & research institutions, vibrant incubation & start-up ecosystem, strong tec-preneurship base are the strong indicators paving way for Innovation Roadmap of Punjab. Further, multiple initiatives are being taken for stimulating State’s Innovation & Entrepreneurial Ecosystem

Department of Science, Technology and Environment - GoP has launched Mission Innovate Punjab with aim to build robust research and innovation culture for boosting competitiveness, growth, generating quality jobs and achieving SDGs. Punjab State Council for Science & Technology (PSCST) is a state level node of DST - GoI and is spearheading Mission Innovate Punjab to bring Science, Technology & Innovation at forefront for accelerating socio-economic development of the State. DST has appreciated PSCST as a model S&T Council in the country.

To quantify STI Ecosystem vis-a-vis strength and weakness of Punjab, in line with new National STI Policy, PSCST formulated and submitted a proposal on “Strategic Framework for Mapping and Strengthening STI Ecosystem of Punjab”. Department of Science & Technology, Govt. of India has sanctioned the project and urged PSCST to build STI Indicator Framework to serve as baseline document for emulation by other states Hence, the model framework has been developed by PSCST and present STI Observatory is being setup as a single-window portal to capture, manage, and ease the flow of knowledge among stakeholders.
