STI Data Architecture Framework
Data Architecture Framework defines components of Science, Technology & Innovation
The Science Technology Innovation Observatory Punjab is Country’s first-of-its-kind state-level Knowledge Gateway
supported by Department of Science & Technology, Government of India. The platform showcases STI Ecosystem of the
State viz. infrastructure, human resources, policies, programs, investment etc. captured from key STI partners viz. Public & Private Research Organizations, Universities, Development Departments, Industries, NGOs etc. It is a single stop digital
platform which highlights holistic data on 70+ STI indicators encompassing the entire pipeline viz. Education, Research,
Innovation & Commercialization as well as reflects accomplishments, achievements and available expertise within the State.

The Open Access State Nodal Repository also features dedicated resource pooling platform viz. Equipment Pool, Technology Platforms, Researchers’ Network, IP Portfolio etc. to be leveraged by different stakeholders. The Observatory, in future, envisages to plug in and feed the National STI Observatory to be set up by O/o Principal Scientific Advisor to Govt. of India and Department of Science & Technology, Govt. of India as a part of new National STI Policy.
