A greater emphasis is being given to development and deployment of indigenous technologies to achieve a larger goal of ‘Atma Nirbhar Bharat’ for sustainable and inclusive growth of the Nation. Region-wise Facilitating Platforms are required for collating and showcasing technologies indigenously developed with potential for deployment and commercialization. Technology Platform – Punjab has been set up with this vision, to augment and create systematic inter-linkages for leveraging technological strengths of higher education institution, research institutions, universities, CSIR labs & scientific organizations having presence in Punjab to partner with industry, early stage entrepreneurs, start-ups, private players for access to technology and innovations. It is holistic Initiative to build Innovation Eco-System Punjab and will also feature States’ Technology Need Assessment. It will provide access to technologies required for developmental needs, to address socio economic challenges, unprecedented disasters, improve productivity, emerging future technologies for further networking for acquisition, deployment and diffusion.
