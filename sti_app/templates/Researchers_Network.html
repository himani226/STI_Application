Researchers Network

Punjab has large network of research institutions, universities and STI organizations, wherein researchers are working in varied sectoral domains. This platform showcases portfolios of scientist, academicians and researchers working across Punjab for priority research leads, addressing state’s grand challenges, industrial tech. solutions, programs for socio-economic development etc. This digital platform offers easy access to pool of experts for forging partnerships for collaborative programs, sharing research expertise with scholars, Govt. and Industry as well as providing vision for future roadmap development for state of Punjab. The platform also recognizes and applauds the researchers who have excelled in their respective domains at International & National front. The efforts would be made to link this network to researchers’ network at National and Global levels.
